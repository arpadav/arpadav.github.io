    Diff EMA Risk

    \[ R(t_{n - \nu}) = - |x(t_n)| - \left( \frac{dx(t_n)}{dt} \right)^{2} \]

    where

    \[ x(t_n) = EMA_{p}(t_n) - EMA_{q}(t_{n}) \]

    where \(p > q\), and immediate risk is where \(\nu = 0\) and predicted risk of historical data is where \(\nu > 0\)
